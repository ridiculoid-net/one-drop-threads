How One Drop Threads works

One Drop Threads is a single-edition apparel studio. Each design releases once, printed in the size you choose, and never reprinted.

  • Single-edition: every tee is sold once.
  • Your size: pick your size at checkout.
  • On-demand: Printful fulfills & ships worldwide.
  • Secure checkout: Stripe + Apple Pay / Google Pay.

01 · Pick a drop

03 · Stripe checkout

Pay with card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.

04 · Retired forever

Once purchased, the drop is immediately marked SOLD.
